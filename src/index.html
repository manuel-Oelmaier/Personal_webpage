Principles:

Social Responsibility

I believe technology should be built with intention and impact in mind. As AI becomes increasingly integrated into daily life, I’m committed to developing systems that are safe, transparent, and aligned with human values. My work is driven by the idea that engineers have a responsibility to consider not just what we can build, but what we should build—and how it affects people and society. Books like The Age of Surveillance Capitalism remind us that Change is not inherently good (nor bad).

Development

With a strong foundation in computer science, I’m passionate about building reliable, scalable, and thoughtful software. I enjoy working across the stack—whether it’s crafting clean backend architectures, designing intuitive interfaces, or exploring the latest in AI and machine learning. As I grow as a developer, I strive to write maintainable, human-friendly code that empowers others and leads to meaningful, high-quality products.

Lifelong Learning

Technology changes fast, and staying curious is one of my core principles. I continually explore new tools, frameworks, and ideas—whether through building side projects, studying academic concepts, or experimenting with emerging technologies like LLMs and deep learning. For me, learning isn’t a goal but a habit, and every project is an opportunity to deepen my understanding and improve.
