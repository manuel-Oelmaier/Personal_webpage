Principles

Pragmatism

I approach software development with a balance of aiming high and being realistic . My priority is delivering real, tangible value within realistic timeframes. My focus is on pragmatic iteration: choosing simple solutions that just work, only adding complexity when it genuinely pays off. This means I prioritize new features and improving with user feedback, over chasing perfection.

Software Craftsmanship

With a strong foundation in computer science, I’m passionate about building reliable, scalable, and thoughtful software. I enjoy working across the stack, whether it’s crafting clean backend architectures, designing intuitive interfaces, or integrating the latest ML/AI models. As I grow as a developer, I strive to write maintainable, human-friendly code that empowers others and leads to meaningful, high-quality products.

Lifelong Learning

Technology changes fast, and staying curious is my engine for growth . I continually explore new tools, frameworks, and ideas—whether through building side projects (like ManuChess) or studying academic concepts (like LLMs and transformer architectures). For me, learning isn't a goal but a hands-on habit , ensuring I'm constantly adapting and turning new knowledge into practical, high-impact skills.
